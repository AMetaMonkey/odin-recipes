Homemade Mushy Peas

Description

Why buy store mushy peas? When you can buy store garden peas and make your own!

Ingredients

  • 500g of garden peas
  • 25g butter
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour garden peas to a shallow pan and cover with water.
  2. Bring this to a boil on a medium to high heat, then cover the pan and allow the pears to simmer for 3 minutes.
  3. Drain the peas and transfer to a bowl.
  4. Add butter, salt and pepper and mash until comfortable.
