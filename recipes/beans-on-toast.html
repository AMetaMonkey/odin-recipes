Beans on Toast

Description

Need a quick morning snack? I have got you covered, this recipe is so easy a monkey could follow it!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • A can of baked beans

Steps

  1. Place the bread into a toaster and wait for it to cook
  2. Pour a can of baked beans into a cooking pot and heat up on a low heat, this should take 3-4 minutes
  3. Place you toast on a plate, then get pour the baked beans ontop.
